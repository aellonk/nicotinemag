ARTIST FEATURE

Works

by

Mel Arzamarski

Mel Arzamarski (born 1990) is an American painter based in New Haven, CT. Her mystery-laden and metaphorical paintings are focused on introspection with a sense of dry humor. Her influences are pulled from film, 18th century Baroque painting, and Surrealism.
