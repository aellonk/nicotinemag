ARTIST FEATURE

Flores para Paloma

by

Stefhany Y. Lozano

From the artist: “This is a series I did in my last months of pregnancy expecting my daughter. At the time it was very difficult to work on my paintings, I was very sensitive to the smell of the paint and I couldn’t reach the surface of the paper because I was so big. I wanted to keep working and colored pencils were the best solution I could find. It was also a new technique for me so I was very happy to give it a go. I started this series of drawings from home, where several people were bringing flowers to welcome Paloma to this world. These characters are inspired by Egyptian drawings and paintings, especially the positions of the bodies bringing offerings to their gods as well as some of the geometric elements that can be found in the architecture and decor of Egyptian houses and palaces.”

Stefhany currently lives and works in Leipzig, Germany. She has since transformed this series into a self-published zine. It is available for purchase here: https://lorohouse.bigcartel.com/product/flowers-for-paloma

Flores para Paloma, colored pencil on paper, 2020
