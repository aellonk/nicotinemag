ARTIST PROFILE

Gundröw

Image

Maker

From the artist:

“I started this artistic project when I decided to group existential themes in a fable with my own photographs and decors. The visual story I am telling is the condensed life of a person who feels lost in the immensity of the sky and very attached to the earth. This lonely character is trying to feel alive, to love, but ends up disappearing mysteriously. This small fable repeats itself indefinitely, in a series of photos on my Instagram account and which are all accompanied by a small philosophic and poetic text as a legend. This is done so that if we assemble each post, it creates a small virtual book. My Instagram page is a sort of large story which can be browsed.”

“My photographs explore the sky which is always a strange blue, deserts, rooms always filled with neon pink light and chlorine-filled pools. My fable is a contemporary visual fable hanging between the neo-futurism of the 60's, desert acid trips in the 70's and California motels. The character who is staged is a young girl (who represents me) with long red hair. Different models often double for this character. We almost never see her face entirely so that we can identify ourselves with her. Sometimes she meets other strange characters who are hybrid beings - part human, part alien. The photographic treatment is always the same wether the model is a man, woman or other, and the wardrobe is always disturbing. Some objects I make have a real role in the narration and are almost characters on their own. It's by meeting them on her journey, that the young girl starts to question herself…”
