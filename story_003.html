FASHION STORY

Taken Bodies, The Valium Series of Luca

Photography by

Felipe Huertas

From the photographer

“Taken Bodies, The valium series of Luca” comes from Huerta’s personal project entitled “PUTAS ZORRAS.” “PUTAS ZORRAS” speaks to the controversial yet popular way in which women are pictured in the “macho” society of Latin America. Huerta subverts this notion by photographing male bodies with an untraditional gaze.

Photographed by Felipe Huertas
Styled by: Paula Molina
Hair and Makeup: Andrea Abimeri
Model: Luca Ateca
Art Direction: Luca Ateca and Felipe Huertas
