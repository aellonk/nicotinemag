ARTIST FEATURE

Works

by

Emma Larsson

I’m a tireless explorer.

If I’m comfortable and know too much I get bored and tiered. The levels I’m seeking must be unknown to me. 

To me painting is about playing and exploring freely.

If I try to put some words together to determine my work it would be these:

Liquid. Curious. Intuitive. Contemplation. Fast execution. Poetic. The wordless. Nature’s wonders. Playful. Rhythm. Colors. Meaning seeker. Longing. Freedom. Energy. Shapes.


I’m using several techniques and materials. From drawing to painting, from sculpturing to create collages.

My watercolors and other works are in a constant flow. 

It’s the material and me, but also something else. A certain presence from a hidden force.

I don’t like to analyze too much.

There is something very valuable in the secret.


Larsson’s work is currently on view at Simard Bilodeau Contemporary in Los Angeles until December 18th.
